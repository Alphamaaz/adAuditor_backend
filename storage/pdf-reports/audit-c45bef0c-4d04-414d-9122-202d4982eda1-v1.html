MEDIUMOPP-001

No Google brand campaign detected — brand traffic is unprotected

What is happening: None of your 27 Google campaign(s) appear to target branded keywords. Without a dedicated brand campaign, competitors can bid on your brand name and capture high-intent searches that should convert at a fraction of your non-brand CPA. Brand campaigns typically achieve 5–10× better CTR and ROAS than non-brand.

Estimated business impact: Unprotected brand searches allow competitors to capture your highest-intent traffic at no cost to you.

Confidence: medium. Ease of implementation: easy.
  1. Create a dedicated branded keyword campaign with exact and phrase match variants of your brand name.
  2. Set bids high enough to maintain brand impression share above 90%.
  3. Add competitor brand terms to a separate observation campaign to monitor intent traffic.

Many active Google keywords are getting zero impressions

What is happening: 1168 active keyword(s) (100% of active keywords) received zero impressions in the data period. Common causes: bid below first page, low search volume, disapproval, or keyword-level policy restrictions.

Estimated business impact: Zero-impression active keywords inflate account clutter, mask Quality Score problems, and can indicate disapproved ads that need immediate attention.
