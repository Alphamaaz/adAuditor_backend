Executive summary

What this audit found

The single most important finding in this audit: PKR 7,089 of proven delivery (84% of account spend) is blocked until this ad clears review. Restoring it is the highest leverage action in the account.

The real story: A proven ad is blocked by Meta policy review — the lead one drove 90% of all results. The account is constrained by approval status, not media buying: restoring delivery is the highest leverage move, and no budget or bid change can outperform it. Spend is also leaking to GB the budget cannot compete in — the root cause behind the zero conversion campaigns. The fix is correcting the location targeting, not pausing the campaigns.

Lead issue: Meta ad "New Engagement Ad" is disapproved and blocking delivery. The ad "New Engagement Ad" in "New Engagement Campaign" is Disapproved — a hard delivery stop. It carries PKR 7,089 of spend (84% of the account). It drove 181 of the account's 201 results (90%) before the block. A disapproved ad cannot serve until it is fixed and re reviewed, so no bid, budget, or audience change can outperform restoring it. Policy reason reported: Non Functional Landing Page.

Why it matters: Findings are ranked by leverage — the most damaging issue leads (severity and root cause gravity first, then confidence and recoverable spend), not simply the biggest dollar figure.

Next move: Start with the first issue in this report, then re run the audit after the next full reporting cycle to measure the delta.
